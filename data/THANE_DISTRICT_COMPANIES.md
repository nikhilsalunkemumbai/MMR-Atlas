- **Wagle Estate (Ashar IT Park / MIDC):** The primary engine of Thane's IT sector, hosting high-growth tech (AjnaLens) and large BPO/Analytics firms (Teleperformance, Ugam).

- **Ghodbunder Road:** Premium commercial corridor attracting diverse IT and consulting firms.
