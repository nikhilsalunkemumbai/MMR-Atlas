## Key Security Focus for Professionals:
- **BFSI Sector (1-14, 36-39):** Strong emphasis on compliance, fraud prevention, and robust SOC operations. Strong infrastructure hardening and GPO skills are highly valuable here.
- **IT/ITES & GCCs (15-25):** These companies run massive internal security programs and often manage security for their clients. His 10+ years experience is directly applicable.
- **Digital-First & Telecom (26-35):** Focus on application security, cloud security, and data privacy. Reliance Jio, in particular, has a huge network to secure.
- **Manufacturing & Infrastructure (40-44):** Growing need for Industrial Control Systems (ICS) security and traditional enterprise IT security, leveraging his deep infra background.
