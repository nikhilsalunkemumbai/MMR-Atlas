## Node Summary:
- **Airoli (Gigaplex/Mindspace):** The "Global Hub" with the highest concentration of MNCs (IBM, Accenture, Cognizant).
- **Ghansoli (DAKC/RCP):** The "Enterprise Core" dominated by the Reliance ecosystem and large IT firms like Hexaware.
- **Mahape (MBP/Technocity):** The "Software Corridor" featuring a mix of established IT engineering (Mastek) and niche SaaS (Bigsun).
- **Vashi (Infotech Park):** The "Pioneer Node" for IT in Navi Mumbai, hosting firms like 3i Infotech and local success stories like factoHR.
- **Turbhe/Juinagar (Raheja Mindspace):** A premium emerging hub for BFSI tech (IDFC First) and high-end software services.
